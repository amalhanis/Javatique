Modern Use & Conservation

Modern designers mix traditional motifs with contemporary styles, bringing batik onto runways and into everyday fashion. Education programs, museums, and UNESCO recognition help conserve techniques and cultural meaning.


  • UNESCO – Batik, a Masterpiece of the Oral and Intangible Heritage of Humanity
