How Javanese Batik Is Made



This video shows how Javanese batik is made from drawing with hot wax to dyeing the cloth and revealing the final design. It’s a calm, detailed process that turns simple fabric into beautiful art.


Why the technique matters !

The wax-resist process creates crisp outlines and layered colors. Hand-drawn batik takes much longer and is considered artisanal; stamped batik allows pattern-wide use while keeping motifs alive in everyday fashion.
